THE ROAD NOT TAKEN

ARTIST

ROBERT FROST

TITLE

THE ROAD NOT TAKEN

PROJECT

LYRICS VIDEO

Two roads diverged...

THE ROAD NOT TAKEN is a famous poem written by Robert Frost about life choices and the melancholy and uncertainty that can result from it.

Through this video which accompanies the beautiful and emotional lyrics of Robert Frost, I wanted to give my personal interpretation of it, mainly through images with nature as a protagonist seen from the perspective of a lonely traveller. Even though the images are mostly realistic, I tried to mantain a slight element of magic and timelessness throughout the video and to get the audience as immersed into the landscapes as I was while shooting them. We constantly find ourselves in front of divergent paths that might lead onto comnpletely different journeys.

The important thing is not which path we choose but how we eventually go with it.
