2025 SUPERBOWL

Taste unites the states

Doritos chips have become an important part of the american iconic Super Bowl event. But is it just a question of having something to snack during the event? Hasn’t food always been something that brings people together from different cultures, social backgrounds and ideas, exactly like the Super Bowl event?

Since for the first time American Brand Doritos partners with Mexican Sauce Brand Cholula, what greater opportunity is there to create a campaign that celebrates this incredible partnership and the ability of food to bring people together despite their rivalry and different beliefs. Because let’s face it:

Who can resist Doritos x Cholula?
