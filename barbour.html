WINTER WEAR ADV

It could only be... barbour

In their visual communication Barbour portrays itself mainly as a summer wear brand by setting most of the commercials in the countryside during green outdoor seasons, which has become an iconic imagery for the brand and the brand lovers. However, after a research I found out that one of the most searched google keywords related to the brand is “Barbour Winter Jacket.”

Why not pay homage to the huge variety of Barbour’s product catalog by creating a commercial that is set entirely during winter season and that shows the resistance and practicality that Barbour Winter Jackets provide?
