It's getting late

Elton John is one of the most popular pop music artists which ever lived and she shaped this genre like few other's. Despite being well established among gen X and Baby Boomers, many people among Gen Z don't know the songs of this artist. This is when I decided that I wanted to pay tribute to this prolific artist who has created iconic songs as GOODBYE YELLOW BRICK ROAD, ROCKETMAN, BENNY AND THE JETS and I'M STILL STANDING.

For his 50th anniversary since his debut, I wanted to create a restyling of one of his old songs, all in a style that reflects the naive and nostalgic 70s vibe when the song first came out, but that at the same time meets the Graphic taste of the new generations.
