PRODUCT LAUNCH

Power starts with your feet!

MISCHIEF is a young boundary-pushing and unconventional brand known for blending art and consumer products in ways that challenge the norm. In 2024 they launched 2 x 4 boots, the first shoes on the market which are made almost entirely out of rubber sole. In our modern day consumeristic and fetishizing society where people tend to buy shoes only for their great looks, MISCHIEF challenges their audience and launches a campaign that highlights the shoes functionality and comfort over their aesthetic features.

2x4 boots becomes the everyday everyman option designed specifically for those who seek the ultimate foot protection against any undesired agents lying on the street such as slippery water, mud and many others.
