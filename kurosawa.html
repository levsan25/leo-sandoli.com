A SITE FOR THE SAMURAI OF MOVIES

The artist website for Akira Kurosawa, one of the most groundbreaking filmmakers, a key influence for George Lucas, Sergio Leone, Steven Spielberg, Martin Scorsese, and many more. He drew inspiration from Western classics such as Shakespeare and, in return, greatly influenced Western movie directors, demonstrating the reciprocal influence between Japanese and Western cinema. His pioneering techniques include axial cuts, long takes with deep focus, and the use of weather and nature as integral storytelling elements.

This website wants to be a site that gives a chance to young cinema and art lovers to discover this important artist, from his filmography to the colorful storyboards created for the most important movie scenes.
