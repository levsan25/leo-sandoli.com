DOLLS SHORT MOVIE

It's a burning memory

Dolls is a short film that explores themes of jealousy, fear, and hatred towards a person who is different. In this case, it is conveyed through dolls in a child’s room during the 50s. Once they understand that one doll is more advanced and that they can’t compete with her, they set up a treacherous plan to get rid of the problem once and for all.
