VISUAL IDENTITY

You frame it!

Every year Naba comitions 3rd year students in Graphic Design and Art Direction courses of their own accademy to redesign the visual identity of the Naba Communication awards. This annual ceremony awards students exceptional projects in different areas of communication design such as Ux Design, photography and Advertising. The brief includs a video trailer and the design of the trophy that the winner of each category receives.

Over the time spent at Naba, all the students undergo significant changes in their professional and personal life. In the rush that comes with the academic projects and events, students don't have the proper time to stop for a moment and see how they've truly changed throughour their studies at Naba. But there are these exceptional moments when you pause the work and see the difference between "now" and "then". These moments come when the work has been appreciated...
